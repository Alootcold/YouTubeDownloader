## Key Features

### Download YouTube Videos
- Download videos from YouTube with just a URL
- Support for standard and short URLs (youtu.be)

### Quality Options
- Multiple quality choices: 360p, 480p, 720p, 1080p
- Auto-select best available quality
- Video and audio merged automatically

### User-Friendly Interface
- Clean and modern WinUI 3 design
- Sidebar navigation for easy access
- Real-time download progress tracking
- Download history and management

### Built-in Tools
- Integrated yt-dlp for reliable parsing
- Integrated ffmpeg for video processing
- No additional software required

### Privacy First
- No personal data collection
- All processing done locally
- Your downloads saved where you choose

---

## System Requirements

- Windows 10 (version 1809) or later
- Windows 11
- Internet connection for downloading
